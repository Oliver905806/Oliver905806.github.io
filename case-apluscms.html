About A Plus Ventures

For several years, operational processes were handled primarily through workflows, spreadsheets, and fragmented documentation systems. While the firm sought to transition toward digital transformation, an earlier in-house system developed during a previous internship period was not fully functional and could not support structured case management. This operational gap created the need for a centralized, scalable, and reliable enterprise system which lead to the development and deployment of AplusCMS.

Database Design

The AplusCMS database follows a modular relational architecture consisting of three core domains: Client Management, Case Processing, and Office Workflow Management.

The client entity acts as the primary root table, linking businesses and cases. Cases form the transactional core of the system, connecting services, receipts, and internal office workflows.
